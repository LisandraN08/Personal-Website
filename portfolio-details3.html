RICEisNICE Application

The RICEisNICE Application is a desktop POS application that aims to record all customer orders. Because RICEisNICE uses a pre-order system, buyers are not required to pay when ordering. Therefore, in addition to the order feature (to record orders), there is a reminder feature to remind the owner of RICEisNICE to provide reminders to customers who have not made transactions H-1 before the ordering date. In addition, there is also a dashboard feature for data analysis purposes (customers who make the most purchases, food that is most frequently bought, custom profit graphs based on the desired time frame of the owner) so that the owner can make the best decisions based on the results of the data analysis.
